Our Story

Founded in 1890, Classic Timepieces began as a small watch repair shop in London. Through generations of master craftsmen, we've grown into a premier destination for vintage pocket watches while maintaining our commitment to quality and tradition.

Each timepiece in our collection tells a unique story of craftsmanship and elegance, carefully selected and restored by our expert horologists.
